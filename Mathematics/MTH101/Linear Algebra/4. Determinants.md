#### Expansion

***By Rows:***
> Let $1 \leq k \leq n$ 
> Then, 
> 	$\det(A) = \sum_{l=1}^{n}(-1)^{k+1}\cdot a_{kl}\cdot \det(A_{kl})$

***By Columns:***
> Let $1 \leq k \leq n$
> Then, 
> 	$\det(A) = \sum^{n}_{l=1}(-1)^{k+1} \cdot a_{lk}\cdot \det(A_{lk})$

##### Example
$$
A = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & 2 \\
4 & -1 & 5 \\
2 & 6 & 8
\end{pmatrix}
$$
We will expand along the first column
$$
A_{11} = \begin{pmatrix}
-1 & 5 \\
6 & 8
\end{pmatrix}
,\quad A_{21} = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & 2 \\
6 & 8
\end{pmatrix}
, \quad A_{31} = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & 2 \\
-1 & 5
\end{pmatrix}


$$
